# Social Media Templates

**Purpose:** Platform-specific templates for civic engagement content  
**Platforms:** Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn

---

## Twitter/X Templates

### Thread Format (10-15 tweets)

```
Tweet 1 (Hook):
[Attention-grabbing statement or question - max 280 chars]

Tweet 2 (Context):
[Background/context]

Tweet 3 (The Problem):
[What's at stake]

Tweet 4 (The Evidence):
[Key fact with source]

Tweet 5 (Why It Matters):
[Local impact]

Tweet 6 (What's Happening):
[Current status/upcoming decision]

Tweet 7 (Who's Involved):
[Key players - neutral framing]

Tweet 8 (What Citizens Are Doing):
[Coalition action]

Tweet 9 (Call to Action):
[Specific ask]

Tweet 10 (Resources):
[Link to more info]

Hashtags: #AlachuaWater #ProtectMillCreekSink #LocalGov #WaterProtection
```

### Single Tweet Templates

**Meeting Alert:**
```
MEETING ALERT: [Body] meets [Day] at [Time] to discuss [Topic].

Key item: [Brief description]

Public comment available. Details: [Link]

#AlachuaWater #LocalGov
```

**Decision Announcement:**
```
BREAKING: [Body] voted [X-X] to [approve/deny] [Project/Policy].

What this means: [One sentence impact]

Next steps: [What happens now]

Full analysis: [Link]
```

**Call to Action:**
```
YOUR VOICE MATTERS

[Body] will vote on [Issue] on [Date].

What you can do:
- Submit comment by [Deadline]
- Attend hearing at [Location]
- Share this with neighbors

Details: [Link]
```

**Fact Share:**
```
DID YOU KNOW?

Water entering Mill Creek Sink reaches Hornsby Spring in just 12 days.

That's our drinking water. No filter. No second chance.

Source: [Dye trace study]

#WaterProtection #AlachuaWater
```

## Facebook Templates

### Standard Post (300-500 words)

```
[HEADLINE IN CAPS OR BOLD]

[Opening hook - 1-2 sentences that grab attention]

[2-3 paragraphs explaining the situation in accessible language]

WHY THIS MATTERS:
[Bullet points on local impact]

WHAT YOU CAN DO:
1. [Action 1]
2. [Action 2]
3. [Action 3]

[Closing sentence - empowering, not doom-and-gloom]

Share this post to spread the word.

[Link to more information]

#AlachuaWater #ProtectMillCreekSink #CommunityAction
```

[Date] | [Time] | [Location]

Why attend:
- [Reason 1]
- [Reason 2]
- [Reason 3]

Can't make it? Here's how to participate remotely:
[Options]

RSVP/Details: [Link]

### Victory/Update Post

```

[Brief explanation]

- Attended meetings
- Submitted comments
- Shared information
- Stayed engaged

The work continues. Next up: [What's next]

Stay informed: [Link to subscribe]
```

## Content Guidelines

### Do:
- Lead with the most compelling information
- Use plain language (8th grade reading level)
- Include specific calls to action
- Cite sources for factual claims
- Use local angles and personal stories
- Include relevant hashtags
- Tag relevant accounts when appropriate

### Don't:
- Use partisan framing (left/right, Democrat/Republican)
- Make personal attacks on individuals
- Overstate or sensationalize
- Use jargon without explanation
- Post without proofreading
- Forget accessibility (alt text, captions)

### Platform-Specific Notes

**Twitter/X:**
- Threads perform well for complex topics
- Quote tweets for commentary
- Use polls for engagement
- Pin important tweets

**Facebook:**
- Longer posts acceptable
- Images increase engagement
- Events feature useful for hearings
- Groups for community building

**Instagram:**
- Visual-first platform
- Stories for timely updates
- Reels for broader reach
- Carousel posts for education

**LinkedIn:**
- Professional tone
- Thought leadership angle
- Tag organizations and officials
- Good for reaching professionals

## Image Suggestions

### Types of Visuals

- **Maps:** Show location of Mill Creek Sink, development sites
- **Infographics:** 12-day travel time, development scale
- **Photos:** Springs, sinkholes, community meetings
- **Charts:** Voting records, timeline of events
- **Quote graphics:** Key statements from officials, experts

### Accessibility

- Always include alt text
- Use high contrast colors
- Avoid text-heavy images
- Provide captions for videos
